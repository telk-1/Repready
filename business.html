FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can we get set up?
Same day. We send you a company invite link. Employees click it, answer a few questions about their goals, and have their first personalised programme within 60 seconds. No IT involvement, no integration, no delay.
What if employees have very different fitness levels?
PTrAIn adapts completely to each individual — complete beginners to elite athletes. A sedentary office worker and an experienced gym-goer on the same team both get a programme built specifically for them. Nobody gets a generic plan.
Do employees need a gym membership?
No. PTrAIn works for gym, home, bodyweight, CrossFit, Hyrox, yoga and sport-specific training. If they have nothing at home, it builds an effective bodyweight programme. No equipment needed.
What data does our HR team see?
HR receives anonymous aggregate data only — active users, sessions logged, programmes generated, weekly trends. Individual employee health and fitness data is never visible to employers. Happy to discuss a Data Processing Agreement for your legal team.
How does billing work?
Monthly invoice based on active employee accounts. No setup fees. Scale up or down at any time. Cancel with 30 days notice. No long contracts.
What happens after the free trial?
At the end of your 2-week trial we'll share your engagement report and discuss next steps. No automatic charges, no pressure. You decide if you want to continue based on the data.
