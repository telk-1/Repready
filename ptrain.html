    Questions
    Is PTrAIn just for gym regulars?
    No — it's built for anyone. Tell it your age, goals, and experience level and it adapts. Complete beginners welcome.
    How does billing work?
    Monthly subscription, cancel anytime. No contracts, no hidden fees. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again.
    What happens to my plans if I cancel?
    You keep access until the end of your billing period. History stays saved.
    Is there a free plan with no card needed?
    Yes — Starter gives you 1 plan a month, forever free, no card required.
    I'm a PT — is Coach right for me?
    Absolutely. 50 plans a month, client management, branded exports. Most PTs see ROI in the first week.

    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: June 2026

    PTrAIn ("we", "us") is operated by Tel Kershaw, a UK-based certified personal trainer. This policy explains how we handle your data.

    What we collect

    When you generate a plan, we collect the information you enter (name, age, fitness details). If you provide your email address, we store it to send you updates about PTrAIn. We do not sell your data to third parties.

    Email marketing

    If you provide your email, it is stored securely via EmailOctopus. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying "unsubscribe" to any email.

    Payments

    Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

    Analytics

    We may use session analytics tools to understand how users interact with PTrAIn. No personally identifiable information is shared with analytics providers.

    Your rights

    Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at telkershaw@icloud.com to exercise these rights.
