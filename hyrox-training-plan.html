Training for Hyrox is
not just going to the gym.

Hyrox punishes athletes who only lift or only run. Your programme needs compromised running — running on tired legs after strength stations — plus the specific movements:

  • SkiErg, sled push and pull conditioning
  • Burpee broad jumps and wall balls under fatigue
  • Farmers carries and sandbag lunges
  • Interval running programmed between strength work
  • Progressive volume as race day approaches

PTrAIn programmes all of it based on your current level — whether it's your first Hyrox or you're chasing a PB time.
